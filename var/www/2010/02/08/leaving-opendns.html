Leaving OpenDNS

Today is my last day at OpenDNS.  I’m proud to have been here almost two years (a personal record!), prouder still of what we accomplished while I was here, and confident that they’ll continue to stick it to ISP DNS and remake enterprise content filtering in the cloud.  I am without any doubt a far better engineer for having worked here.

What’s next?  A friend and I are starting something developer focused because that’s the type of customer I love serving and the type of software I love writing.  Stay tuned for more details.
