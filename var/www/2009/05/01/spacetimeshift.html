It’s almost the first Saturday in May, which means it’s almost time to drink and gamble celebrate my Kentucky heritage.  Here’s the setup I will be using to observe the race at 6:24pm as Eastern Daylight Savings Time intends.

It’s good, ol’ fashioned Kentucky cable TV, split and sent to both the TV there and an old TiVo.  The TiVo outputs to and is controlled by Mike Malone’s Slingbox, which is being displayed on my TV in San Francisco via Malone’s DVI-to-HDMI cable.  Thanks, Mike!
